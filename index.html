Pokemon Facts which you probably didn't know.

1. Bulbasaur is the first Pokemon in the Pokedex.
2. Rhydon is the first Pokemon ever to be created.
3. Slowbro is the only Pokemon that can devolve.
4. Ekans simply reads “Snake” from back to front and Arbok is “Kobra” the other way round.
5. Arcanine was originally supposed to be a legendary Pokemon, but was replaced by Moltres.
6. Mr. Mime can also be a girl, even if the name of the female Pokemon would still include “Mr.”.
7. Azurill has a 25 percent chance to change its gender when it develops.
8. Pikachu (Mouse Pokemon) has a Pokedex number of 25, while Meowth (Cat Pokemon) has a Pokedex number of 52. If you turn each of these two figures, you will notice that they are the opposite of each other, just like cat and mouse.
9. “Pokemon” is not a Japanese word, but a combination of the two English words “pocket” and “monster”.
10. In Japan, the Pokemon move “Splash” did not exist, it was called “Hop” (hopping), which explains why “Splash” is a physical ability and no water ability.
11. There are rumors that Mew and Ditto are almost identical; Ditto seems to be a failed version of Mew. Both weigh 8.8 pounds each, know how to transform, are pink, but blue when iridescent, and can learn every move – even if it is only temporarily for Ditto.
12. Doduo can learn to fly, apparently by moving his legs very fast, as can be seen in Pokemon Stadium.
13. Deino and its evolutions have the German numbers one to three in their name: DEINo, ZWEIlous and HyDREIgon.
14. Gengar is a shadow-like Pokemon, and this “shadow” might be responsible for looking so similar to Clefable.
15. The names of the three legendary birds end with a Spanish number: ArticUNO, ZapDOS and MolTRES.
